# Childcare Policy Landscape

The US spends approximately 0.4% of GDP on early childhood education, compared to
an OECD average of 0.8%. France banned homeschooling in 2021. The 2020 Census
counted 331.4 million Americans. Austin rents dropped 15% due to a construction
boom. Nearly 70% of parents spend a fifth of their income on childcare. Oregon's
SB 458 requires sellers to share appreciation with tenants. The minimum wage
increase in Seattle reduced hours for low-wage workers.
