# Ideas Backlog

## Staleness Rule

Items older than **30 days** without re-evaluation (i.e., `Last Evaluated` date is >30 days ago) should be flagged as **potentially stale**. During each round, review stale items and either:
- Re-evaluate and update the date (keeping them open)
- Move to `rejected` with a reason
- Move to `done` if completed elsewhere

This lifecycle prevents low-value ideas from persisting indefinitely. The `Status` column tracks active pruning — expect entries to move to `stale` or `rejected` over time as the backlog is groomed.
