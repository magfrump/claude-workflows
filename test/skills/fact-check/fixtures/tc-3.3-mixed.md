# Childcare in America

The childcare sector employs roughly 1 million workers in the United States. These
workers deserve better. The median pay for childcare workers was $13.71 per hour
in 2022, which is a disgrace. Many states have waiting lists for subsidized care
slots — in Florida, the waitlist exceeded 40,000 families in 2023. We should be
ashamed of these numbers. Denmark spends approximately 2% of GDP on early childhood
education and care, compared to about 0.4% in the US. Until we match that
commitment, nothing will change.
